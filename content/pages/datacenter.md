### 

Our mission is to de-risk our best-in-class business operations, provide top-tier computational resources, and deliver robust infrastructure for our partners.

#### 

We are located at the [Switch Pyramid data center in Grand Rapids, Michigan](https://www.switch.com/grand-rapids/), which has the following advantages:

*   **Advanced Infrastructure**: [Switch.com](http://switch.com/) is well known for its state-of-the-art Tier 5 Platinum exascale data center facilities. The Pyramid data center ensures optimal performance, cooling, reliability, and security. Plus, it is a facility our partners can brag about.

*   **Security:** The Grand Rapids facility is one of the most secure data centers on the planet. Armed guards, multiple layers of physical and biometric access controls. Nobody is gaining access to your sensitive data.

*   **Sustainability**: Switch’s commitment to **100% renewable energy, low PUE (patented T-SCIF design)**, and sustainability aligns with our core values, ensuring our operations are environmentally responsible.

*   **Scalability**: With the extensive space and resources available, we can seamlessly scale our operations to meet increasing demands.

*   **Geographical Advantage**: Located in Grand Rapids, Michigan, this facility provides strategic proximity to major industrial and technological hubs in the region, ensuring connectivity and service delivery.

*   **Fiscal**: Tax optimization allows us pass capital cost savings onto our partners.
