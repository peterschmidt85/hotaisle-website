Hot Aisle Inc.

Thanks for stopping by. Nice to meet you.

Hot Aisle Inc. was founded by [Jon Stevens](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jon-s-stevens/) and [Clint Armstrong](https://www.linkedin.com/in/clint-armstrong/) in October 2023. Both of us are hands-on technical experts with decades of experience. We have been working together for over 5 years, successfully deploying large-scale compute across 9 data centers (and counting). We come from open-source backgrounds and prioritize ethics, transparency, and honesty in our business dealings.

After months of considering names for the company, Jon thought about common terms in a data center and "Hot Aisle" dawned on him as a fantastic name. While "Cold Aisle" was another obvious choice, he felt that "Hot Aisle" better represents the high-performance compute services we offer.

We are gratefully supported by [Joseph Lubin](https://consensys.io/about/joseph-lubin-founder-of-consensys) and [Mesh](https://mesh.xyz/).
