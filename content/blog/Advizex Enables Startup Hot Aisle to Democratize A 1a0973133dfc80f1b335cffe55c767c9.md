This video shows how Advizex, a Dell Titanium Solution Provider Partner, enabled Hot Aisle realize its mission to democratize compute. Hot Aisle is a startup GPU-aaS provider that sought to build out and deploy a supercomputer center based on the Dell XE9680 with AMD MI300x GPUs. By democratizing compute, Hot Aisle is facilitating access to AI and supercomputing capability to developers to improve AI overall. In partnering with Hot Aisle, Advizex brought its full suite of capabilities which include: Solutions architecture, delivery and implementation services, personnel and staff augmentation and supply chain integration.
