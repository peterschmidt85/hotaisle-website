# **Chief Growth Officer – Marketing, Sales & Business Development Lead**

**Location:** 100% Remote

**Company:** Hot Aisle Inc.

[hello@hotaisle.ai](mailto:hello@hotaisle.ai)

[https://hotaisle.ai](https://hotaisle.ai/)

[https://linkedin.com/company/hotaisle/](https://linkedin.com/company/hotaisle/)

**Overview:** Hot Aisle Inc. is looking for an experienced and visionary CGO - marketing, sales, and business development leader. This role is critical in our journey as a rapidly growing company in the AI and HPC infrastructure space. We seek a true unicorn with a proven ability to get things done, wear multiple hats, and drive significant growth through strategic marketing, sales enablement, and business development initiatives. We realize that this role is a big ask, but we know that there are driven individuals who enjoy this type of challenge. In this high-impact role, you’ll shape our success story, working closely with the key members of management including founders, board and advisors, and take ownership of the business’s success.

**What You’ll Do:**

- **Strategic Leadership:** Take direct responsibility for Hot Aisle’s marketing and business development strategy. Design and execute a go-to-market plan that targets key industries and markets, builds strong partner ecosystems, and maximizes growth opportunities.
- **Sales Enablement & Execution:** Go beyond traditional marketing; drive a high-impact sales enablement program that builds connections, identifies influencers, and unblocks roadblocks. Build strategies to get partner C-Suite and Sales leaders committed to the vision, gaining support and resources.
- **Event Activation & VIP Engagement:** Own the execution of exclusive, high-touch events. Curate VIP guest lists, design spaces for trustworthy private conversations, create captivating experiences, and build unforgettable moments that generate buzz and lasting impact. From pre-event meetings to post-event follow-ups, you’ll be at the center of relationship building.
- **Business Development & Partner Ecosystem Building:** Cultivate a powerful network, bringing together the right people to drive business conversations and accelerate growth. You’ll act as a connector across industries, crafting joint solutions and aligning partner intellectual property, for mutually beneficial relationships.
- **End-to-End Marketing Activation:** Develop and execute multi-channel GTM strategies, from sales campaign strategy and customer-targeted events to film production for content creation. Elevate the Hot Aisle brand through innovative and high-engagement marketing efforts, fostering relationships and influencing key stakeholders.
- **Technology-Driven Insights & Execution:** Leverage the latest technology tools like Hubspot, Leadfeeder, and Koala to optimize and measure the effectiveness of your marketing and sales initiatives. Demonstrate expertise in building, deploying, and monitoring ad campaigns, analyzing their performance, and driving actionable insights to refine strategies and maximize engagement.
- **Innovation:** Champion innovative approaches to reach target markets and break into new channels. We’re looking for someone with the courage to challenge the status quo and introduce bold new ideas to drive exceptional results.
- **Customer and Market Insight:** Act as a market intelligence hub, understanding industry trends, and translating them into actionable sales, marketing, and GTM strategies. Maintain a pulse on market shifts and customer needs to ensure Hot Aisle is positioned as a thought leader.
- **Value-Driven Engagement:** At Hot Aisle, we believe trust, honesty, and ethics are fundamental to every relationship we build and decision we make. You’ll embody and promote these values in every message and interaction—whether with partners, clients, or internal teams. By fostering transparent and ethical engagement, you’ll help create an environment where trust leads to genuine, sustainable growth and lasting partnerships.

**Qualifications:**

- **Proven Track Record:** At least 7+ years of hands-on experience. A history of building and executing successful GTM strategies, marketing campaigns, and sales enablement initiatives in technology, preferably within the AI, HPC, or enterprise hardware industry.
- **Communication Skills:** Extensive experience with communication tools such as Discord, Slack, and similar platforms, demonstrating an ability to maintain a high level of responsiveness. Obsessive about staying connected, prioritizing quick responses and clear communication to keep all stakeholders aligned and informed. Effective at juggling multiple priorities and conversations across various channels, ensuring that nothing falls through the cracks. Communication that fosters transparency, builds trust. Manage multiple initiatives and relationships seamlessly. Coordinate and execute tasks across a dynamic, remote-first team, keeping everything moving forward while maintaining attention to detail.
- **Event & Engagement Expertise:** Demonstrated success in curating and hosting exclusive events that create impact, build relationships, and elevate brand perception.
- **Relationship-Driven Approach:** Skilled at building high-level relationships, from C-Suite executives to industry influencers, with the ability to engage partners, open doors, and create meaningful connections.
- **Technological Savvy:** Proficiency with platforms like Hubspot, Leadfeeder, Koala, and other CRM and analytics tools. Comfortable with ad management, performance monitoring, and data analysis to continuously improve campaign performance and drive results.
- **Entrepreneurial Mindset:** You thrive in dynamic, high-growth environments and can pivot quickly. You take ownership, drive initiatives, and act with the accountability of a founder.
- **Sales & Marketing Enablement Skills:** Ability to create and support multi-route GTM strategies, design sales messaging, and deliver tailored solutions to drive growth and meet targets.
- **Business Development and Ecosystem Building:** History of establishing and nurturing relationships with senior-level executives, decision-makers, and partners to drive business conversations and strategic alliances. Known for acting as a "connector" within industry networks, identifying and bringing together influential figures to create opportunities and accelerate growth.

**What We Offer:**

- **100% Remote Work:** You’ll travel to conferences and customer engagements, but we do not anticipate having an office. Hot Aisle has built a connected, collaborative remote culture that values clear communication, mutual support, and continuous learning. You’ll be joining a team where your input is valued, and your contributions are directly recognized.
- **Comprehensive Benefits:** 100% medical coverage, competitive salary, and equity in a fast-growing company.
- **Commission:** As a core part of our team, you’ll be encouraged to hit your targets and benefit from our success.
- **Founding Experience:** Work directly with the founders and play a pivotal role in shaping Hot Aisle’s future.

**Apply if You Are:** A self-starter, strategic thinker, relationship builder, and expert executor with a knack for leveraging technology to drive results. This role is ideal for someone passionate about making a tangible impact and creating something extraordinary in the world of AI and HPC. If you’re ready to be part of an adventure where your contributions are recognized and valued, we want to meet you. Please reach out to us with a brief introduction and resume: [hello@hotaisle.ai](mailto:hello@hotaisle.ai)
