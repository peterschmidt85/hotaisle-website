# **Hot Aisle Achieves SOC 2 Type 1 Compliance**

We’re excited to announce that Hot Aisle has officially completed our SOC 2 Type 1 audit. This marks a major milestone in our journey to build a trusted, secure, and compliant cloud infrastructure platform.

SOC 2 (System and Organization Controls) is a widely recognized standard developed by the AICPA for evaluating a service provider’s ability to securely manage customer data. The Type 1 audit specifically evaluates the design of our internal controls as of a single point in time.

For us, this isn’t just a checkbox. It’s proof that we’ve built strong processes from day one, designed to protect our customers’ data, ensure operational integrity, and meet the rigorous standards of enterprise and regulated industries.

### **What’s Next?**

SOC 2 Type 2 is already underway and on track for completion within the next three months. While Type 1 demonstrates that controls exist, Type 2 validates that those controls are operating effectively over time. We’re confident in our systems and look forward to achieving this next milestone.

### **Special Thanks**

We couldn’t have done this without the help of our partners:

- **Sprinto** – their compliance automation platform made the process seamless and efficient.
- **ATOM** – our audit partner, who worked closely with us to evaluate and document our controls.

If you’re considering SOC 2 compliance and want a referral to a trusted partner, we’re happy to give a referral.

Hot Aisle is committed to making secure, high-performance compute accessible, without compromise. This is just the beginning.
